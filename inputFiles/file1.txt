The upcoming movie Oppenheimer, directed by Christopher Nolan, is based on the 2005 book American Prometheus: The Triumph and Tragedy of J. Robert Oppenheimer by Kai Bird and Martin J. Sherwin. The film stars Cillian Murphy as Oppenheimer, the theoretical physicist who helped develop the first nuclear weapons.
The film is set during World War II, and it follows Oppenheimer as he leads the Manhattan Project, the research and development undertaking that created the first nuclear weapons. The film also explores the moral and ethical implications of Oppenheimer's work, as he grapples with the knowledge that his creation could be used to destroy the world.
